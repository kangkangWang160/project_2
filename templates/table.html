    Poker Hand Rankings

    Royal Flush
    A, K, Q, J, 10, all the same suit.
    Example: A♠ K♠ Q♠ J♠ 10♠
    Straight Flush
    Five cards in a sequence, all the same suit.
    Example: 9♥ 8♥ 7♥ 6♥ 5♥
    Four of a Kind
    Four cards of the same rank.
    Example: 4♣ 4♠ 4♦ 4♥ 9♠
    Full House
    Three of a kind and a pair.
    Example: 8♠ 8♦ 8♣ 2♥ 2♠
    Flush
    Any five cards of the same suit.
    Example: K♣ 10♣ 7♣ 6♣ 2♣
    Straight
    Five cards in a sequence.
    Example: 5♠ 4♦ 3♣ 2♥ A♠
    Three of a Kind
    Three cards of the same rank.
    Example: Q♠ Q♦ Q♣ 7♣ 2♥
    Two Pair
    Two different pairs.
    Example: J♠ J♦ 8♣ 8♥ 3♠
    One Pair
    Two cards of the same rank.
    Example: 6♣ 6♠ Q♦ 9♥ 3♣
    High Card
    When you haven't made any of the hands above.
    Example: A♣ J♦ 8♠ 6♥ 3♣
